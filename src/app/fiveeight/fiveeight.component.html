30 OUT OF 30


The year 2008 took Super 30 to the ‘Pinnacle of Glory’. All its students made it to the IIT-JEE, an achievement that came after a lot of hard work and one-point devotion. As the results came, there was a wave of jubilation at Anand’s house, with successful students and their overwhelmed parents queuing up to pay their gratitude. After all, many of them had never thought of the metamorphosis, which Super 30 brought about in their lives in just a year.



Chief Minister Nitish Kumar shared the success of the Super 30 by participating in the felicitation programme. Several banks, companies and organizations came forward to felicitate Anand Kumar and successful students for their outstanding performance.
