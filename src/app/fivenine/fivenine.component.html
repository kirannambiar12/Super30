IIMA Lecture

Anand Kumar (extreme left) in a seminar at IIM-A.



The lecture delivered by Anand Kumar at India's top business management school, thel Indian School of Management, Ahmedabad (IIM-A) was appreciated by all. He visited IIM-A on the invitation of Prof Anil Gupta, Head, Rural Management for delivering a lecture. During his lecture, he showed a short film and detailed the procedure by which he shortlists and trains the underprivileged students from rural background. The audience enjoyed a session with him. In the interactive session, they asked several questions related to Super 30 and the reasons behind its phenomenal success.
