Education without border


March 30, 2011, "Education is the answer to world's pressing problems," Anand Kumar, mathematician and founder of the Super 30, said at a programme ‘Education Without Border’, organised by Higher Technology College, Dubai.UAE. Other prominent speakers at the conference included former British Prime Minister Tony Blair, Nobel laureate Albert Fert, UNSECO head Irina Bokova and Kenyan Prime Minister Raila Odinga.
