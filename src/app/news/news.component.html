Anand Kumar is dedicated to mathematical education since his early student life. It has always been his dream to become a good mathematics teacher and researcher. During his life, he faced a lot of difficulties and hurdles, but adversities only helped him grow stronger. It has been a long journey for Anand Kumar since 1992, when his love for mathematics and a keen desire to contribute something significant in the field of mathematics gave birth to Ramanujan School of Mathematics. Initially, it was just a platform for math lovers to share ideas and thoughts. Gradually, it expanded to help the needy children. As years rolled by, his approach got well appreciated.
