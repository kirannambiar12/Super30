Olivia Arthur a photographer from London


November 15th, 2013, Olivia Arthur, a renowned photographer from London, was in Patna to shoot mathematician Anand Kumar’s Super 30 in its totality.Recipient of many awards, including Royal Photographic Society’s Odden Award and Laureate Photographe of Paris, Arthur shot not just at the Super 30, but also went to shoot at the homes of some students.
