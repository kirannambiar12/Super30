Japanese Beauty at Super 30


Anand Kumar receiving Norika Fujiwara

Former Miss Japan and famous actress Norika Fujiwara visited Ramanujan Society of Mathematics for a documentary film shoot. The Japanese film team arrived in Patna to make a movie on Super 30. The film crew began shooting almost instantly at the institute and Dekuli village in Gaurichak from where Alok Kumar, one of the students of the current batch, came. They also interviewed teachers of the Super 30.
