Patna Book Fair


Anand Kumar was at the Patna Book Fair, an annual event that draws all the book lovers to the historic Gandhi Maidan in the midst of Patna. Anand was also invited there for the release of a book on mathematics, written by a young writer from the State. That he did. But before he could do that, his mere presence created a bigger splash, and a problem for the organisers, who had to arrange additional security to manage a huge crowd of youth and elderly, men and women, which assembled there to have a glimpse of Anand or shake hands with him.
