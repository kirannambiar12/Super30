The 60th foundation day of the IIT, Kharagpur


Super 30 founder Anand Kumar and Magsaysay Award winner Prakash Amte at a function organised at New Delhi to mark the 60th foundation day of the IIT, Kharagpur on Friday. The IIT, Kharagpur Alumni Association "IIT Foundation" had called both Amte and Anand Kumar as the main speakers on the topic "How to change society through education"
