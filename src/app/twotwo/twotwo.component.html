Wise Conference in Doha


November 3, 2011, Anand Kumar delivered a lecture at an international conference ‘’world innovation summit for education’’ organised at Doha, Qatar. Russian Youth Engineering Society president Dr. Alexander Karpov and Poland's famous biology teacher Mr. Grzegorz Lorek are also with him on the stage.
